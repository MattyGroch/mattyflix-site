Best practices

  • Prefer Original quality for direct play; lower quality only if you see buffering.
  • Use Ethernet or fast Wi‑Fi for the most stable connection.
  • Set default language in Plex: Settings → Audio & Subtitles → Preferred audio language: English; Subtitle mode: Only forced or Only for foreign audio.
  • Check subtitles from the playback menu and pick your preferred track.

Troubleshooting

Buffering

Lower playback quality or check your connection speed.

Can’t connect

Make sure you’re signed into the correct Plex account. Try signing out and back in.

No sound

Pick the correct audio track in the player’s settings.

FAQ

How do I get an invite code? +

I share invite codes directly. If you don’t have one yet, reach out to me and I’ll send one over.

Is Mattyflix free? +

Yep. It’s a personal, non-commercial project. Donations help cover storage, bandwidth, and upgrades.

What quality can I expect? +

Direct play whenever possible (including 4K where available). For best results, use a wired connection or strong Wi‑Fi.
